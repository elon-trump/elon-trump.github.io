Welcome To Our Organization

Vishwas Lok Sewa Sansthan

About Our Organization

Vishwas Lok Sewa Sansthan (VLSS) is a registered non-governmental organization based in the culturally rich and vibrant state of Rajasthan, India. Founded in 2023, with a deep-rooted commitment to serving our communities, VLSS has been tirelessly working to bring about positive and sustainable changes in the lives of the underprivileged and marginalized sections of society.



Our Focus Areas

At VLSS, our core focus areas encompass the critical domains of Rural Education, Women Empowerment, Skill Development, Healthcare Accessibility, Environmental Sustainability, and Social Causes. We firmly believe that addressing these pillars is fundamental to creating a more equitable and prosperous society.








Our Vision

To create a society where every individual, regardless of their background, has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for personal and community growth.

We envision a world where empowerment, equality, and environmental sustainability are at the core of societal values.

Our Mission

At Vishwas Lok Sewa Sansthan, our mission is to work relentlessly towards realizing our vision. We strive to uplift underprivileged communities by providing holistic support in education, skill development, healthcare, and women's empowerment.

We are committed to preserving our environment and actively engaging in social causes that drive positive change.

Our mission is to Build a brighter, more inclusive future for all, one step at a time.





Organizational Structure



Governing Body

VLSS is governed by a dedicated and democratically elected Governing Body composed of passionate individuals who bring diverse expertise and experiences to the table. This esteemed body, elected periodically, ensures that our organization operates with transparency, accountability, and the highest standards of governance.

Decentralized Management

Our organizational structure is designed for maximum efficiency and effectiveness. It operates under a decentralized management system, led by the Chairman. Regular monthly review and planning meetings are held, attended by experts in various fields, monitor program performance and provide strategic insights for our initiatives. This structure ensures effective leadership and collaboration for our mission's success.

Collaborative Approach

To enhance our impact, VLSS actively collaborates with eminent experts in fields such as education, healthcare, environment, and social work. This collaboration allows us to tap into the wealth of knowledge and experience, ensuring that our efforts are both innovative and impactful.
